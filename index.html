About the Office

The Office of Legal Services serves as the University's legal counsel and provides legal advice, policy guidance, and representation in legal matters affecting the institution. The office promotes compliance, good governance, and protection of the University's legal interests.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who may request legal assistance?

University officials, employees, students and authorized offices may request legal services subject to University policies.

How do I request legal assistance?

Submit a written request together with the necessary supporting documents to the Office of Legal Services.

Does the Office provide legal advice to students?

Legal assistance is provided only within the scope of the University's policies and official functions.

Where is the Office located?

University of Southern Mindanao Administration Building Kabacan, Cotabato
